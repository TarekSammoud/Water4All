Who are we ?

Water4All is lead by a small team of motivated ESPRIT students that met through Enactus ESB, club for social entrepreneurship.

The Mission

Dangerous roads, polluted water, fatigue, illnesses, that’s what many communites have to go through to access «Clean» Water This vital element, the one that no living being on this earth could live without, is either not accessible to thousands of people, or it contaminated putting many lives at risk

We at Water4All have made it our mission to help these communities by providing a source of clean drinkable water and minimizing the risk and effort of sterilizing water in underdeveloped areas of the country
